Anglea's Receipie For the best cinnamon Roles

Ingredients

For the dough

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoon east
  • 1 egg plus 1 yolk
  • 3 cup of bread flour

For the filling

  • 2/3 dark brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

    Instruction

  1. mix the milk with yeast and sugar ,eggs
  2. melt the butter and add the mixture
